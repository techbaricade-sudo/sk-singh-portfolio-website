About Me

Mr. Shivam Kumar Singh is an educator, IT professional, author, entrepreneur, and digital creator dedicated to empowering education through technology and innovation. With expertise in computer education, digital solutions, educational leadership, and creative development, he has successfully contributed to both academic and technological fields.

He is currently serving as the Incharge of the IT Department at Sr. Sec. Delhi Public School, Patuaha, Saharsa (affiliated to CBSE New Delhi up to 10+2). He is also the Managing Director of Tech Baricade Group and Academic Director of Lakshya Academy, where he actively works toward enhancing educational standards and digital learning systems.

As an author, he has published multiple books focusing on social awareness, human emotions, and educational inspiration. His mission bridges traditional education with modern technological advancements.
